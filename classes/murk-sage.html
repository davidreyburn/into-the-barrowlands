The Murk Sage

You were born where light dies. The shadows taught you their language, how to bend them, speak through them, show others what hides in the dark corners of their minds. In sunlight you're fragile, half-blind. But when darkness falls, you are sovereign. The nightmares you weave are real enough to kill, and the shadows never forget a friend.

HP: 6+Constitution
Damage: d6
Load: 6+STR.

Class Mechanics: Shadow Weaving

You shape darkness itself. Shadows are not merely the absence of light to you, they are substance, tool, ally, and weapon.

In darkness, you thrive. Create nightmares that manifest as tangible horrors, cloak allies in shadow to hide them from sight, step between dark places as if they were doorways. The shadows obey your will.

In light, you suffer. Direct sunlight weakens you, and your magic fades when exposed to bright illumination. You are fragile where others are strong, powerful where others are blind.

But there is a deeper cost: the more you dwell in shadow, the less you remember the light. Your connection to the world of the living grows distant. Warmth, hope, joy, these become foreign concepts. The shadows are loyal, but they are also hungry.

Names

Corvith, Nyx, Umbra, Shade, Veil, Dusk, Mirk, Tenebris

Morrigan, Noctis, Raven, Whisper, Eclipse, Shadow, Gloom, Vesper

Look

Choose one for each:

Eyes: Milky white, All black, Reflect no light, Never blink

Hair: Raven black, Shadow-dark, Wispy and thin, Shaved

Body: Gaunt, Hunched, Slight, Unsettling

Clothes: Ragged cloak, Shadow-woven robes, Tattered wrappings, Feathered mantle

Starting Moves

You start with these moves:

Shadowcraft

When you weave illusions from living shadow in darkness (dim light or worse), describe what you create and roll+WIS. ✴On a 10+, the illusion is vivid and believable; choose 2. ✴On a 7-9, it manifests but is flawed; choose 1:

  • It moves and reacts as you will it
  • It produces convincing sound
  • It can be touched without revealing itself
  • It lasts until you dismiss it or leave the area

In bright light, you cannot use this move.

Nightmare Projection

When you gaze into someone's eyes and dredge up their fears, roll+WIS. ✴On a 10+, you learn their deepest fear and can manifest a shadowy vision of it; they must Defy Danger (WIS) or freeze/flee. ✴On a 7-9, you glimpse a fear and create a weak echo; they are unsettled but not paralyzed.

Requires at least dim lighting.

Eyes Adapt

You see perfectly in darkness. Mundane shadows never hinder you, and you can navigate lightless places as if they were lit.

Darkness Lingers

Even in bright light, small shadows persist, behind the eyes, beneath the tongue, in the heart.

When you have no other darkness, you may draw upon these inner shadows to use Shadowcraft at -1 ongoing. This is exhausting; the GM will tell you when you've pushed too far.

Alignment

Choose an alignment:

Chaotic

Use your illusions to sow confusion and turn enemies against each other.

Neutral

Reveal a hidden truth by showing someone their fear or desire.

Evil

Use darkness to harm an innocent or exploit their terror.

Bonds

Fill in the name of one of your companions in at least one:

_____ does not fear the dark, and that intrigues me.

I have shown _____ a nightmare they cannot forget.

_____ trusts me despite the shadows I command.

I owe _____ a debt; they pulled me from the darkness when I was lost.

Gear

Your Load is 6+STR.

You start with:

  • Ragged cloak (worn, 0 weight)
  • Sage Thought talisman, a skull-shaped icon (0 weight, bound to you)
  • Moon Marker, a small carved stone (0 weight)
  • Ritual dagger (hand, 1 weight)
  • Dungeon rations (5 uses, ration, 1 weight)

Choose your defense:

  • Leather armor (1 armor, worn, 1 weight)
  • Shadow-woven cloth (0 armor, ignore the clumsy tag in darkness, 0 weight)

Choose one:

  • 3 vials of ink (useful for marking or obscuring, 0 weight)
  • A worn book of nightmares (describes common fears, +1 to Nightmare Projection when consulted, 1 weight)
  • Lantern and oil (when you need to create darkness by extinguishing it, 1 weight)

Advanced Moves

When you gain a level from 2-5, choose from these moves:

Cloak of Murk

You can wrap yourself in shadow, becoming difficult to perceive. While in darkness, you have +1 armor and enemies attacking you must Discern Realities to see you clearly.

Shadow Step

When you step into one shadow and emerge from another nearby, roll+DEX. ✴On a 10+, you arrive exactly where you intended. ✴On a 7-9, you arrive but draw unwanted attention or leave something behind.

Whispers in the Dark

Your illusions can speak with your voice, or with voices you've heard before. They can hold conversations, though they know only what you know.

Devouring Shade

When your shadow touches someone in darkness, you may drain their strength. Roll+WIS. ✴On a 10+, deal your damage and they are weakened (-1 forward to STR/DEX). ✴On a 7-9, deal your damage but your shadow recoils, you cannot use Shadowcraft until you rest.

The Dark is My Ally

You are never hindered by darkness. You can act in pitch black as if it were daylight, and your allies within arm's reach share this benefit.

Shade Walker

When traveling through areas of natural darkness or shadow, you leave no tracks and make no sound. Take +1 ongoing to avoid detection while in darkness.

Advanced Moves

When you gain a level from 6-10, choose from these moves or the level 2-5 moves:

Birth Terrors

Your Nightmare Projection can now affect multiple targets at once (up to your WIS). They all see the same horror or individual nightmares, your choice.

Umbral Form

When you dissolve into shadow, roll+WIS. ✴On a 10+, you become intangible shadow; physical attacks pass through you, and you can slip through cracks. Hold 3. ✴On a 7-9, as above but hold 1.

Spend hold 1-for-1 to maintain the form through danger or exertion. When hold is gone, you reform.

Consume the Light

When you snuff out a light source with a gesture, roll+WIS. ✴On a 10+, the light is gone and darkness spreads. ✴On a 7-9, the light flickers and dies, but something notices.

Sage's Thought

Requires: Sage Thought talisman

Your skull talisman now houses an illusion tied to your mind. It can act independently within sight range, scout, and relay what it perceives. If destroyed, you cannot use Shadowcraft until you carve a new one (requires a day's work and a fresh skull).

Moon Marker

Requires: Moon Marker

You track the sun's position even in darkness or underground. You always know when night falls, how long until dawn, and whether you are in true darkness or merely dim light. When your Moon Marker is broken, you lose this sense until you repair it under a full moon.

Master of Shadows

Your Shadowcraft illusions are now indistinguishable from reality. On a 10+, they are perfect, even touch and taste are convincing.

Living Nightmare

When you use Nightmare Projection on a 10+, you may choose to make the nightmare linger. It will haunt them for days, appearing in their dreams and at the edge of their vision. They take -1 ongoing until they confront and overcome the fear you've shown them.

In darkness, you are unmatched. In light, you are fragile, but the shadows behind every eye offer refuge.
