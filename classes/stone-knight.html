The Stone Knight

You were born in the deep mines where the air is poison and the dark is absolute. You learned to carry weight that would crush others, breathe fumes that would kill, and stand unmoved when fear comes crawling. Your pickaxe has tasted stone and bone alike. When the depths try to break you, you simply endure.

HP: 10+Constitution
Damage: d10
Load: 12+STR instead of the normal calculation.

Class Mechanics: Stone Blood

Years in the deep mines have changed you. You are immune to poison and disease—the toxic fumes and cursed minerals that kill others merely flow through your veins like water.

You are unmovable when you choose to be. Plant your feet, and nothing short of supernatural force can shift you. You carry burdens that would break others, stand in places that would crush lesser warriors.

But the stone in your blood comes with a price: you heal slowly when resting naturally. Your body is dense, unyielding, resistant to both harm and recovery. Magical healing works normally, but time alone mends your wounds with the patience of mountains.

You endure. That is what you do. That is what you are.

Starting Moves

You start with these moves:

Stone-Born

You were forged in the deep places. You have an uncanny tolerance for toxins and poisons:

  • Resist natural poisons entirely
  • When you Defy Danger against magical or alchemical toxins, take +1
  • You can breathe thin air and endure noxious fumes that would choke others

Bearer of Burdens

Your Load is 12+STR instead of the normal calculation.

You can carry immense weight without slowing. When others would be crushed or exhausted by a load, you simply endure.

Stoic Resolve

Fear and supernatural dread do not touch you easily.

When you would Defy Danger against fear, terror, or mind-affecting magic from creatures of darkness, roll+CON instead of +WIS.

On a 10+, you are utterly unmoved and gain +1 forward against the source of the fear.

Armored in Duty

You ignore the clumsy tag on armor. Heavy plate feels like a second skin.

Pickaxe Mastery

Your miner's pickaxe (close, forceful, 2 weight) is a weapon of war in your hands. It deals your class damage and can puncture armor: when you Hack and Slash with it, on a 10+ you may choose to ignore 2 armor instead of dealing damage.

Alignment

Gear

Your Load is 12+STR.

You start with:

  • Heavy plate armor (2 armor, worn, clumsy [ignored], 3 weight)
  • Miner's pickaxe (close, forceful, 2 weight)
  • Helmet with lantern affixed (provides light, 1 weight)
  • Dungeon rations (5 uses, ration, 1 weight)

Choose one memento from the mines:

  • Uncut gemstone worth 50 coin (0 weight, reminds you of home)
  • Ancestor's shield (+1 armor when you Defend, 2 weight)
  • Map of the deep mines (useful for navigation underground, 0 weight)

Choose one:

  • Coil of rope (100 feet, 1 weight)
  • Satchel of mining tools (1 weight, +1 when working with stone)
  • Second pickaxe to give to an ally (close, forceful, 2 weight)

Advanced Moves

When you gain a level from 2-5, choose from these moves:

Tunnelborn Senses

You can sense structural weaknesses in stone and earth. When you Discern Realities underground or in structures of stone, take +1 and add this question to the list:

  • Where is this place weak or likely to collapse?

Iron Lungs

You can hold your breath for ten minutes without strain. Drowning, smoke, and airless voids do not hinder you as they would others.

Unshakeable

When you plant your feet and brace against being moved or knocked down, roll+CON. ✴On a 10+, you are an immovable object; you cannot be forced back or knocked prone. ✴On a 7-9, you hold your ground but take 1d6 damage from the strain.

Gemcutter's Eye

You can identify valuable minerals, gems, and ore at a glance. When you Spout Lore about treasure, metals, or gems, take +1.

When you sell gems or ore, you always get fair value—no merchant can cheat you.

Miner's Resilience

Gain +1 armor when underground or in enclosed stone spaces.

Relentless

When you Hack and Slash, on a 12+ you deal your damage and the enemy is staggered—they take -1 forward.

Advanced Moves

When you gain a level from 6-10, choose from these moves or the level 2-5 moves:

Earthshaker

When you strike the ground with tremendous force (using your pickaxe or gauntleted fist), roll+STR. ✴On a 10+, choose 2. ✴On a 7-9, choose 1:

  • The ground cracks and becomes difficult terrain
  • Enemies nearby are knocked prone
  • A weak structure nearby collapses or crumbles
  • You create a small opening or breach in a wall

Poison Blood

Your blood is so thick with resistance that toxins become inert. You are immune to all poisons, mundane and magical. When something attempts to poison you, it suffers 1d6 damage as your body rejects it violently.

Burden-Bearer's Strength

When you carry someone or something precious to safety despite overwhelming danger, roll+CON. ✴On a 10+, you deliver them unharmed and gain +1 ongoing until you rest. ✴On a 7-9, you deliver them but you are exhausted; take -1 ongoing until you rest.

Lightless Vigil

You have spent so long in the dark that you see in perfect darkness as if it were twilight. You gain darkvision.

Unyielding

When you take your Last Breath, you may choose to remain conscious at 0 HP instead of rolling.

You are dying but awake. You can act, but every move you make brings you closer to death. When you finally rest or receive healing, roll your Last Breath then.

Stone's Endurance

Gain +2 HP per level (retroactive). You're harder to kill than stone.

Living Fortress

Replaces: Armored in Duty

You are clad in stone and iron. Gain +1 armor (stacks with other armor) and you count as heavy cover for allies adjacent to you.

Born in the crushing darkness of the deep mines. Steadfast, toxic-blooded, and unmovable. Where others flee, you remain.
