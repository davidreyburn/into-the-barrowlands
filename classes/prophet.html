The Prophet

Marko, old world god, was decapitated long ago. His mummified head still speaks, to you. Through visions, it shows futures drenched in death and decay. You carry the head and perform the rituals, speaking prophecies no one wants to hear. The dead listen. The living fear. Marko shows you ruin, and you walk toward it anyway.

HP: 6+Constitution
Damage: d6
Load: 6+STR.

Class Mechanics: Marko's Head

You carry Marko's mummified head, the severed remnant of an old world god. Through it, you receive visions of futures drenched in death and decay. The head grants prophecies, but at a cost.

The more you consult Marko, the more he demands. His influence grows with each vision. What begins as guidance becomes compulsion. The line between his will and yours blurs.

The dead listen to Marko's voice when you speak his prophecies. The living fear it. The head shows you ruin, doom, and endings, but knowledge of fate does not always mean the power to change it.

Your relationship with the head shapes your character. Are you his prophet, his vessel, or his prisoner? Every vision brings you closer to the answer.

Starting Moves

You start with these moves:

Marko's Head

You carry the mummified head of Marko, an ancient god long dead. It speaks only to you, revealing visions of futures yet to come, always grim, always true.

The head is bound to you (1 weight). It cannot be permanently lost, it will return by fate's design.

When you consult Marko's head about what is to come, roll+WIS. ✴On a 10+, Marko shows you a clear vision of a coming danger or opportunity; take +1 forward when acting on this knowledge. ✴On a 7-9, the vision is fragmented or disturbing; you learn something useful but also something you wish you hadn't.

The visions are always of death, decay, or doom: Marko sees only the dark paths.

Divine Conduit

You channel the will of a dead god. When you beseech Marko for divine intervention in a moment of need, describe what you ask and roll+WIS. ✴On a 10+, Marko grants your request; the miracle manifests. ✴On a 7-9, he grants it, but choose one:

  • It comes with a grim cost or omen
  • You owe him, he will demand something later
  • The effect is partial or temporary

You may use this once per session without cost. Additional uses require a sacrifice (burn incense, offer blood, speak prophecy).

Prophet's Burden

You see too much. When you witness death, violence, or decay, you do not flinch, you have seen it all before in visions.

Take +1 to Defy Danger against fear or horror. However, you struggle to feel hope or joy; take -1 to Parley when trying to inspire or uplift others.

Marko Effigy

You bear a Marko effigy, a carved icon, one of the only remaining images of the god (0 weight). When you display it openly:

  • Worshippers of old gods recognize and respect (or fear) you
  • You can identify places where Marko was once worshipped
  • Undead and spirits of the dead can sense the god's presence and may react (with reverence or hostility)

Incense Rituals

You carry an incense bag filled with sacred herbs (3 uses, 0 weight). When you burn incense and perform a ritual (10 minutes), choose one:

  • Commune with the Dead: Ask one question of a corpse or spirit; they must answer truthfully
  • Ward Against Decay: Preserve a body or object from rot for one week
  • Cleanse: Remove a minor curse, disease, or corruption from yourself or another

Replenish by gathering rare herbs or purchasing from those who remember the old ways.

Alignment

Choose an alignment:

Lawful

Deliver a prophecy that upholds order or prevents chaos, even if it brings suffering.

Neutral

Speak a hard truth that others need to hear, knowing it will change them.

Evil

Use your prophecies to manipulate someone into a doomed course of action.

Bonds

Marko speaks favorably of _____; they are important to the future.

I am trying to prevent the doom I saw for _____, but I do not know if I can.

Gear

Your Load is 6+STR.

You start with:

  • Marko's mummified head (1 weight, bound to you, speaks prophecy)
  • Marko effigy, carved wood or stone (0 weight, marks you as a prophet)
  • Incense bag (3 uses, 0 weight, for rituals)
  • Ragged prophet's robes (worn, 0 weight)
  • Dungeon rations (5 uses, ration, 1 weight)

Choose one weapon:

  • Gnarled staff (close, two-handed, 1 weight)
  • Ritual blade (hand, 1 weight)
  • Censer on a chain (close, 1 weight, can burn incense while fighting)

Choose one:

  • Scroll of Marko's teachings (useful for converting others, 0 weight)
  • Vial of sacred oil (2 uses, anoint the dying for peaceful death, 0 weight)
  • Shroud (can wrap bodies for respectful burial, 1 weight)

Advanced Moves

When you gain a level from 2-5, choose from these moves:

Death's Herald

Your prophecies become self-fulfilling. When you speak aloud a vision of someone's death, roll+WIS. ✴On a 10+, it will come to pass unless they take drastic action; they know this to be true. ✴On a 7-9, it might come to pass; they are unsettled but not certain.

Once spoken, you cannot take it back.

Through Marko's Eyes

You can see through Marko's head when it is separated from you (up to one mile away). Place it somewhere and gain perfect knowledge of what happens near it, as long as you meditate.

Visions of Ruin

When you study a structure, object, or body, Marko shows you how it will fall, break, or die. Take +1 to Discern Realities regarding:

  • Structural weaknesses
  • Fatal flaws in plans
  • How something will fail

Grim Counsel

When you offer prophecy or advice drawn from Marko's visions, roll+WIS. ✴On a 10+, they believe you; take +1 ongoing when acting together on the prophecy. ✴On a 7-9, they are skeptical but wary; you gain no mechanical benefit but plant doubt.

Deathless Servant

Marko grants you the ability to raise one corpse as a loyal servant (use hireling rules: Loyalty 2, one skill related to their former life). They are lifeless and silent but obey you.

If destroyed, you must perform an incense ritual and consult Marko to raise another.

Speaker for the Dead

When you use Commune with the Dead, you may ask up to 3 questions instead of 1.

Advanced Moves

When you gain a level from 6-10, choose from these moves or the level 2-5 moves:

Marko's Blessing

Once per session, you may speak a prophecy of survival instead of doom. When you do, choose an ally:

  • They gain +1 ongoing for the rest of the session
  • The first time they would die, they survive with 1 HP instead

This is exhausting; you take -1 ongoing until you rest.

Avatar of Decay

When you invoke Marko's aspect of death and decay, roll+WIS. ✴On a 10+, choose 3. ✴On a 7-9, choose 1:

  • Deal 2d8 damage (ignores armor) to a target you can see as they wither
  • Rot all food and water in the area (near range)
  • Cause a structure to decay rapidly (wood splinters, stone cracks)
  • Age a living creature, they take -1 forward to all physical actions

Hear the Unspoken Dead

You can hear the whispers of all dead things around you, bodies, ghosts, bones. This grants you knowledge but is maddening. When you enter a place of death (battlefield, graveyard, tomb), the GM will tell you one secret the dead know.

However, their voices are always present. Take -1 to any roll requiring concentration unless you silence them with incense.

Prophet's Mantle

You are recognized as Marko's chosen. When dealing with:

  • The faithful: They revere or fear you; gain +1 to Parley
  • The dead/undead: They acknowledge your authority; intelligent undead must Defy Danger to attack you
  • The skeptical: They see you as a madman; take -1 to Parley

The Last Vision

When you take your Last Breath, before you roll, Marko shows you one final vision:

Ask the GM one question about the future (after your death). They will answer truthfully. Then roll your Last Breath.

If you survive, you remember the vision, and it will come to pass.

Inevitable End

When you speak a prophecy of doom and it comes true exactly as you described, gain 1 XP.

The bearer of grim futures, speaking truths none wish to hear. Marko sees only ruin, and you are his voice.
