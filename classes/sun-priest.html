The Sun Priest

The sun god Dorvos turned his face away after the northern empire fell. You carry his cinder—an ever-burning coal of holy wood locked in iron—and you walk toward the lowest place in the world. If you can bring light to where it has never touched, maybe he'll come back. Maybe.

HP: 8 + Constitution
Damage: d8
Load: 10 + STR

Names

Aelric, Daveth, Theron, Marius, Castien, Eldric, Sorin, Brennan

Starting Moves

The Sacred Lantern

You bear the Lantern of Dorvos: an iron lantern on a heavy chain, housing an ever-burning cinder of holy wood. It weighs 1, has the close and forceful tags, and you may use STR or WIS when you Hack and Slash with it.

The lantern provides light (near range) and never goes out unless extinguished by powerful magic or divine curse.

All your priestly moves channel through the lantern. Without it, you cannot use Radiant Healing, Holy Fire, or Turn the Darkness.

The Pilgrimage

You begin with 1 Progress. Mark Progress when you move toward your goal (bringing the cinder to the lowest place in the world) or overcome an obstacle on your pilgrimage.

When you reach 10 Progress, you have arrived. The GM will tell you what final trial awaits.

Benefits:

  • At 3 Progress: +1 to Radiant Healing
  • At 6 Progress: +1 armor
  • At 9 Progress: Once per session, reroll a failed roll related to your pilgrimage

Radiant Healing

When you hold the lantern close to a wounded ally and pray for Dorvos's mercy, roll+WIS.

✴On a 10+, restore 1d8+WIS HP; they feel warmth and hope.

✴On a 7-9, restore 1d8 HP, but choose one:

  • The light dims; you can't use lantern moves until you rest
  • You take 1d4 damage channeling too much
  • Dorvos's silence is heavy; take -1 forward to use this move again

Holy Fire

When you invoke the cinder's flame against enemies, describe your manifestation and roll+WIS:

  • Searing Burst: Near range, ignores armor, 1d8 damage
  • Blinding Flash: Close range, enemies Defy Danger or are stunned
  • Pillar of Flame: Near range, 1d10 damage, forceful

✴On a 10+, it manifests as intended.

✴On a 7-9, choose one: light dims, draw attention, or flame spreads unintended.

Turn the Darkness

When you raise the lantern and invoke Dorvos's name against creatures of shadow, undead, or evil, roll+WIS.

✴On a 10+, choose 3. ✴On a 7-9, choose 1:

  • They flee from the light (near range)
  • They cannot approach while you hold the lantern high
  • They take 1d6 damage each moment in the light
  • One creature is banished or destroyed (GM's discretion)

Alignment

Lawful: Uphold a vow or tenet of Dorvos's teachings, even when it costs you.

Good: Use the lantern's light to protect or heal someone, expecting nothing.

Neutral: Make progress on your pilgrimage, regardless of who it helps or harms.

Bonds

Fill in the name of one of your companions in at least one:

  • _____ does not believe Dorvos will return. Their doubt troubles me.
  • I have sworn to _____ that I will see this through, no matter the cost.
  • I saved _____ with the lantern's light. They have walked with me since.
  • _____ lost someone in the battle where Dorvos fell. I carry their grief.
